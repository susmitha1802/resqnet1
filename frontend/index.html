ℹ️ About ResQNet

Built to save lives faster

ResQNet is an academic CSP project developed by a team of 4 students to address the critical gap in disaster relief coordination. The platform bridges the communication barrier between affected communities and relief organizations.

By integrating AI-based priority prediction, real-time maps, and structured volunteer management — ResQNet ensures no request falls through the cracks, even during large-scale disasters.
