Platform Capabilities

Everything You Need in a Crisis

ResQNet is engineered for speed and reliability when seconds matter most.

🤖
AI Prioritization

Machine learning automatically classifies and ranks help requests by severity, ensuring critical cases get immediate attention.

🗺️
Live Map View

Real-time map showing active disasters, volunteer positions, and help requests. Filter by priority, type, or proximity.

📦
Resource Allocation

NGOs manage and deploy food, water, medicine, and equipment. Inventory tracking with real-time allocation matching.

🔔
Instant Alerts

Smart push notifications alert nearby volunteers and NGOs the moment a high-priority request is submitted in their area.

📊
Admin Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards with charts for request types, status trends, volunteer activity, and relief distribution data.

🔍
Duplicate Detection

AI identifies and merges duplicate reports from the same incident, keeping operations clean and preventing redundant deployments.

Simple & Fast

How ResQNet Works

1
Report or Request

Victims submit a help request or disaster report with location, type, and photos in under 2 minutes.

2
AI Analysis

Our AI instantly classifies the request, assigns priority, predicts resources needed, and checks for duplicates.

3
Volunteer Dispatch

Nearest available volunteers are notified. NGOs automatically allocate matching resources from their inventory.

4
Relief Delivered

Volunteers complete tasks, upload proof, and admins verify completion. The victim is notified instantly.
